Six human needs

Every human has six humans needs that influences their behaviour and decisions.

While we all have the same six needs, we can differ in the way we value each of these needs, and we can also differ in our beliefs about what it takes to meet each need. The way we go about meeting these needs can be divided into resourceful and unresourceful appraoches. By being aware of these needs and how they might influence our behaviour, we can improve how ways we meet these needs by making decisions that support ourselves.
